DINOTOPIA

This website's main goal is to provide educational content on Dinosaurs through the use of beautifully illustrated images by James Gurney to immerse users in an interactive environment.

This website was created for the DECO1400 project. All images were created by James Gurney's, and are from his Dinotopia series. The first book, A Land Apart from Time, where the images were primarily taken from can be found here.
